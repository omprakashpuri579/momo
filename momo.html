Our Story

A Taste of
Nepal in Helsinki

My name is Omprakash Puri. Born and raised in Nepal, now studying and living in Helsinki — I brought the one thing that reminds me of home: my mother's momo recipe.

Every dumpling is hand-folded the traditional Nepali way, filled with seasoned chicken, steamed or fried to perfection. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just the real thing.

I sell momo three days a week in Helsinki. When it's gone — it's gone. That's how it works in Nepal. That's how it works here.

— Omprakash, your momo guy

"Every fold is a memory.
Every bite is Nepal."
